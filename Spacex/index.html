FALCON-9

Falcon 9 is a rocket that can carry cargo and humans into Earth orbit, even reaching the International Space Station.

GOAL OF FALCON-9

Falcon 9 was designed to compete with the Delta family of launchers in that it can lift payloads of up to 8,300 kg (18,300 pounds) to geostationary orbit.

FIRST STAGE

Falcon 9's first stage incorporates nine Merlin engines and aluminum-lithium alloy tanks containing liquid oxygen and rocket-grade kerosene (RP-1) propellant.
